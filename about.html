I am a multidisciplinary digital artist and Official Lens Creator specializing in immersive augmented reality.

Read My Story

My Journey

It started as an experiment. In 2019, I opened Spark AR for the first time just to see if I could put a 3D model of my cat on my head. That weekend project spiraled into an obsession with spatial computing, shaders, and real-time rendering.

Since then, my independent filters have amassed over 2.5 Billion impressions organically. This traction led me to collaborate with agencies and brands worldwide, helping them translate their physical products into digital assets.

My philosophy is simple: AR shouldn't just be a gimmick. It needs to be visually stunning, performant on old devices, and intuitively interactive.

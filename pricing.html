Frequently Asked Questions

Do you publish the filter for us?

Yes! We provide full support for uploading and getting the filter approved through Meta Spark Hub, Snapchat Lens Studio, or TikTok Effect House using your brand's account.

How do revisions work?

Depending on the package, you receive 1 to 3 rounds of revisions. We send you test links so you can try the filter on your own phone before finalizing.

Do I need to provide 3D models?

Not necessarily. We can build custom 3D models for you (included in Pro/Enterprise). If you already have CAD or 3D files of your physical products, we can optimize them for real-time mobile rendering.

What Drives Costs?

1

Custom 3D Modeling vs Pre-made

If we need to model a highly detailed bespoke item (like a specific sneaker) from scratch, it requires significantly more hours than utilizing existing 2D assets.

2

Logic Complexity

A filter that just tracks a face is straightforward. A filter that counts blinks, scores points, and triggers different sound effects based on those points requires custom scripting.

3

Number of Platforms

Porting an Instagram filter to Snapchat or TikTok isn't always a 1-to-1 conversion and requires rebuilding logic in a different engine.
